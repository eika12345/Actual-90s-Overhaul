Projects

Fashion E-commerce Website

https://eikasden.com/

Eika's Den is my attempt of making an e-commerce website. I went for a brutalist/minimalistic style, taking inspiration from Golf Le Fleur.

Portfolio Website (1)

https://www.erikaspetrauskas.com/

Erikas Petrauskas (.com) was my first real portfolio. Heavily inspired by the DIY web aesthetics used in the 1990's and early 2000's. I created it more out of passion for the style rather than some ultra-refined piece of work. Looking back, I used it as my first stepping stone into the massive world of web development.

Portfolio Website (2)

Images

This is another portfolio website I created, heavily focused on animation and sleek aesthetics. I quickly switched from this, as I thought it was too bland and simply lacked any expression (It was then swiftly replaced by erikaspetrauskas.com )

FiveM Roleplay Community & Service Hub

Images

This was a client website made for a GTA V FiveM Server. It featured a store, where you could buy properties and vehicles, a contact form and a forum for the community.
